Arduino Project #4. MnM Culling Machine.
Start Date: May 25th 2018
Completion Date: June 6th 2018
Refactoring/Cleaning: Complete

Device filters out a particular colour of MnM/Skittle/Colour etc.
Put device in learning mode, supply a sample of the colour to be filtered out, supply large quantity of MnMs/Skittles, device will filter out the ones of that colour.

Plans for the hardware component can be found at the original designers site below:
https://howtomechatronics.com/projects/arduino-color-sorter-project/
